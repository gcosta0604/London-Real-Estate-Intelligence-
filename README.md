# London-Real-Estate-Intelligence-
London housing market analysis for the year 2024

**Headline** 

London Housing Market Analysis 2024: Identifying Arbitrage Opportunities in 63,000+ Government Records.

**Executive Summary**

Engineered a data pipeline to ingest, clean, and analyze over 60,000 confirmed real estate transactions from the UK Land Registry. The goal was to quantify the valuation gap between London's premium and emerging districts to guide investment strategy.

**Key Insights**

**Market Inequality:** Identified a 24x valuation spread between London’s most expensive district (Mayfair, £8.3M avg) and its most affordable (Thamesmead, £344k avg).

**Asset Class Trends:** Quantified the premium of Detached properties (£2.3M) versus Flats (£650k), highlighting a 3.5x capital requirement gap.

**Technical Stack:**

**Python (Pandas)**: For ETL processing of 100MB+ raw government data.

**Seaborn/Matplotlib:** For geospatial trend visualization.

**Data Cleaning:** Handled missing values and standardized 3 distinct postcode formats.
